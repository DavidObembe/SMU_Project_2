Alarming Increase:California Traffic Accidents Surge above Pre-pandemic Levels


From our data, the safest cities in terms of number of accidents during the pandemic have been Wyoming(WY), Vermont(VT), Maine(ME) and West Virginia(WV). Whereas the top five cities with the most accidents have been California(CA), Florida(FL), Texas(TX),North Carolina(NC) and South Carolina(SC). In almost all the states, majority of the accidents occurred during the day.

When we compared the data to the same months before the pandemic(from March to June), we observed that for majority of the states(especially the cities with generally low accident counts), there were fewer accidents. However, California and Florida absolutely shattered this rule as California's accidents almost doubled during the pandemic whereas there was a slight increase for Florida.
