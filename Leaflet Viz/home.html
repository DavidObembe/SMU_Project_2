Data Source



Data was obtained from a countrywide car accident dataset which covered 49 states of the USA. The accident data ranged from February 2016 to June 2020, were gotten from two traffic APIs and contained in depth information on weather conditions, time of accident, duration of accident and severity of accident(on a scale of 1-5) â€”all of which were captured by US and state departments of transportation, law enforcement agencies and traffic cameras and sensors.

There were 3.5 million datasets in total within this csv file.
